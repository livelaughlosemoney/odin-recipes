Chicken, Broccoli, Rice Recipe

Description

Chicken, broccoli, and rice is a high-protein, delicious meal that is great for your health and meal prepping. Just prep it once a week and you can heat it up and eat it whenever.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Seasoning (i.e. salt, pepper, cajun seasoning, garlic powder)
  • Olive Oil
  • Broccoli
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Season chicken breast with seasoning.
  2. Marinade it for 30 minutes at least.
  3. Wash and cook rice.
  4. Wash and cut broccoli.
  5. Season broccoli.
  6. Oil baking tray.
  7. Lay broccoli on trays and bake for 12 minutes at 400F.
  8. Remove broccoli, place chicken on tray.
  9. Bake chicken for 20 minutes at 400F.
  10. Eat.
