Salad Recipe

Description

A salad ia a nutritious, easy meal to make on the go or for a nice dinner. It can be convenient for a meal to eat at work and easy to prepare the night before. Personally, I love salads and I eat them all the time.

Ingredients

  • Spring Mix
  • Red Onions
  • Cucumbers
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Olive Oil (or whatever dressing you prefer)
  • And whatever othe vegetables you like

Steps

  1. Wash and chop vegetables.
  2. Put vegetables in a bowl.
  3. Add in salt, pepper, and olive oil.
  4. Mix.
