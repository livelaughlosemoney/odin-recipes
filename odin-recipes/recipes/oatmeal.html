Oatmeal Recipe

Description

Oatmeal is a delicious, easy, nutritious breakfast. You can make it at home, in the office, or wherever you can access water and a microwave. I eat oatmeal everyday.

Ingredients

  1. Quick Oats
  2. Water or Milk
  3. Sugar or Sweetener
  4. Peanut Butter (or PB Fit)
  5. Cinnamon
  6. Berries

Directions

  1. Put oatmeal in bowl.
  2. Pour liquid into bowl.
  3. Microwave for two minutes.
  4. Mix in all dry ingredients.
  5. Add a touch more water.
  6. Microwave for 30 seconds.
  7. Enjoy!
